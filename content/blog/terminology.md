*When commissioning a fursuit, you may encounter some new terminology that might be unfamiliar. This page aims to give some information about common fursuit making terminology.*

## Digitigrade vs. plantigrade

You may have heard these words floating around, as they are quite common when describing the style of a fursuit. In a plantigrade suit, no padding is added to the bodysuit. The shape of the legs will therefore be straight. In a digitigrade suit, the legs are made to resemble animal legs, with stuffing in the feet.
